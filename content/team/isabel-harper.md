Isabel Harper is the Organizing Director for Act on Mass. Isabel first connected with Act on Mass as a volunteer on the stipend reform ballot measure campaign. She is a founding member of True North Action Alliance Boston and serves on the Board of Directors for Just Zero. Isabel studied politican science and environmental studies at Bennington College in Vermont. She is originally from Chattanooga, Tennessee, and now lives in Somerville. She/her/hers
